About Us

Nissi Diabetes Care is a Nurse Practitioner-owned Private Medical Practice located in Amityville, New York.

The Practice is dedicated to treatment and management of all types of diabetes including those using Insulin Pumps, Insulin Sensors, and the latest available technology to provide optimal treatment and care.

  • Our Mission

    To help people with Diabetes achieve optimal control of the disease, minimize complications and extend life.

  • Our Vision

    Transform Diabetes care for better health and prevent spreading of the disease by creating awareness through education.
